The Problem

The homeowner’s problem: Mowing your lawn and removing snow from your driveway can be tedious, time-consuming and inconvenient. Lawn care professionals can be expensive, and finding a quality service can be inconvenient as well.

The teens’ problem: The flexible schedule, relatively high pay, and the ability to manage your own work make lawn care an attractive employment opportunity for teens. However, many struggles to find enough customers that lawn care can replace a more consistently scheduled work option.

Homeowner Solution

Limitless Lawns solves the homeowner’s problem by simplifying the task to a few taps through an app, similarly to how Uber simplified getting taxis. They will receive quality service at a reasonable rate while contributing to their community by buying local. They will also have the option to schedule services, allowing them to connect with one local teen on whom they can rely for regular lawn service.

Teen Solution

We solve the teens’ problem by substantially increasing their customer base and providing clear standards of behavior and practice that will help them build positive reputations. Through regular interactions with their own customers, they will develop soft-skills that will benefit them for the rest of their lives.
